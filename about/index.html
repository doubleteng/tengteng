Teng Teng

Designer · Researcher · Educator

I work across computation, materials, fabrication, and robotics to translate research into built experiments, design prototypes, and teaching. My current interests include multi-material 3D printing, robotic post-tensioned concrete, and programmable matter for architecture and product design.

Research Focus

Computation
Geometry & toolpath design
Computational design, structural patterning, and digital simulations informing fabrication constraints.
Materials
Biomineral & multi-material systems
Material programming for carbon capture, graded stiffness, and tension–compression structures.
Fabrication
Additive and hybrid processes
Scaled trials to full-scale prototypes, post-tensioning strategies, and integrated testing rigs.
Robotics
Robotic processes
Custom end-effectors, sensing, and workflow for reliable repetitive printing and assembly.
Methods
From lab to field
Iterative loops of compute → fabricate → test → refine, with metrics and documentation suitable for teaching.
